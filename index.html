Welcome

Your Time Here Should Feel Like an Escape

At Twelve & West, we believe the salon experience should be different. Step into our plant-filled sanctuary, sink into a vintage velvet chair, and let us take care of the rest. This is your time to breathe, relax, and leave feeling like the best version of yourself.

No rushed appointments. No assembly-line service. Just intentional, personalized care in a space designed to feel like a retreat from the everyday.

Meet Laura

The Heart Behind the Chair

Laura Rawn opened Twelve & West with a simple vision: create a salon that feels nothing like the rest. A space where every detail matters, from the vintage furniture to the carefully curated playlist.

With a passion for colour, an eye for style, and an unwavering belief that everyone deserves to feel beautiful, Laura has built more than a salon. She has built a sanctuary.

When she is not behind the chair, you will probably find her with her dogs, dreaming up new ways to make your next visit even more special.

Laura Rawn, Owner
